Dum Gosht Biryani

Ingredient:

  • 1 kg Cubed Baby Lamb
  • 25 gm Cooking oil
  • 150 gm Yoghurt
  • 100 gm Mint Leaves
  • 50 gm Pure Ghee
  • 100 gm Crispy Fried onions
  • 750 gm Basmati Rice
  • 3 gm Saffron
  • 15 ml Rose Water

Marinade:

  • 75 gm Minced Ginger
  • 75 gm Minced Garlic
  • 50 gm Kashmiri Chilli Powder
  • 30 gm Turmeric Powder
  • 25 gm Garam Masala Powder
  • 25 gm Green Chilli
  • 60 ml Lime Juice
  • Salt-To Taste

Method

Mix the marinade in a large dish. Add Lamb cubes and leave covered in a cool place for 2 hours. Cook this meat in oil for 15 mins. Add yoghurt and cook till the meat is tender. Remove from fire.

Wash rice and boil with required salt till 3/4 th cooked. Drain and keep aside.

Assemble the meat and rice mixture in a pot. Drizzle in hot ghee. Sprinkle saffron, mint leaves from top, cover with foil and put on hotplate and cook for 20 miinutes, Drizzle in the rose water and serve hot garnished with fried onion and mint leaves.
